About Me

I am a passionate and driven BTech student at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam, pursued CSE (Data Science) 2021-2025 batch. My academic journey has ignited my fascination with data science, and I am deeply inspired by the power of data to drive meaningful decisions and solve real-world problems.

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the idea of transforming raw data into actionable insights. Whether it’s analyzing trends, building predictive models, or creating interactive dashboards, I find joy in uncovering the stories hidden within numbers. My coursework and projects have equipped me with a strong foundation in programming, statistics, and machine learning, and I’m constantly exploring new tools and techniques to expand my expertise.

I have a keen interest in data visualization and enjoy crafting compelling dashboards using tools like Tableau and Power BI. I believe effective visualization is key to making data accessible and understandable for everyone, from technical teams to business stakeholders. Additionally, I’m proficient in Python and R for data analysis and have hands-on experience with SQL for database management.
